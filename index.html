Start a piece

Upload an image, or open a blank canvas. Then ask the AI to add, remove, move, recolor, or transform elements. Iterate together.

How this works: the local model can direct two kinds of changes. It can transform your photo as a whole — apply filters, presets (polaroid, noir, moody-forest, cyberpunk…), crop, adjust tone, color, and grain. And it can add vector overlays on top — shapes, frames, labels, annotations. What it cannot do is see or edit specific pixels of your photo (change just the sky, recolor a particular person, remove objects). For placement-sensitive overlays, describe what's in the image.
